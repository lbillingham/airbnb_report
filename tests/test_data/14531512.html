About this listing

The Garden Rooms have been featured in Grand Designs magazine (Sept 2017) the Guardian (August 2014), Scottish Field (July 2014) and GQ magazine (Sept 2014).

You’ll love my place because of the secluded and private walled garden, with exclusive use for guests.

The Garden Rooms is close to Inverleith Park and Edinburgh Botanic Gardens - with super quick access to the City Centre.

We are most suited for couples and business travellers. Young children are welcome.

The space

The Garden Rooms has featured in The Guardian, GQ magazine and Scottish Field. Each publication has reviewed us and believes it to be the ultimate secluded and unique apartment, based near the centre of Edinburgh.

It's super stylish design integrates fantastically in with the listed buildings that surround it. The wifi is super speedy and reliable. There is a brand new Bose bluetooth speaker and we also have a SMART TV with full access to most channels, including Sky Sports and Netflix.

The Botanic Gardens are adjacent and we're very close to Inverleith Park and Stockbridge.

The Garden Rooms has it's own walled garden, with exclusive use for its guests.

The apartment is all on one level, with bi-folding doors opening seamlessly out onto the garden.

There is cupboard space and places to hang clothes/store shoes etc. There is also an ironing board/iron and a hair dryer kept here as well.

The bathroom has an excellent shower, there is always hot water and plenty of towels for you to have.

The kitchen includes: Nespresso machine, toaster, fridge, oven, kettle, induction hob etc. We include nespresso coffee pods, teas, milk and snacks.

I have an outstanding knowledge of excellent restaurants/food outlets in Edinburgh. I'm a bit of a foodie and can recommend some great places to eat.

Guest access

The whole apartment is for you and is not shared with anyone else.

You have your own gate and do not share access with anyone else.

Interaction with guests

I should be able to meet you in person to let you in, show you the apartment and answer any questions. Sometimes, due to your arrival time and what I'm up to, I wont be able to meet you personally and so will arrange to leave keys at the building to collect. You can contact me by phone or on airbnb at anytime during your stay.

Check-in is usually any time after 3pm and check-out times are 11am. However I'm flexible and I will try and accommodate your schedule whenever possible. So with that in mind it's really helpful to know your travel plans so please let me know what time you intend to arrive and depart and by what method of transport when you book.
